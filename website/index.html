openGym

Your workouts. Your weights. Your data.

A free, open-source gym & body-weight tracker. No subscription, no account required, no telemetry — ever.

Get openGym Try the live demo GitHub ★

Everything a training log needs

Built in the gym, not in a boardroom

Every feature exists because it was needed on the gym floor — nothing is there to sell you anything.

▶️

Guided workouts

Knows what day it is, starts today's session, pre-fills your weights from last time, times your rest, spots PRs.

🏋️

1,324 exercises

Searchable library with animated demos, filterable by body part and the equipment you actually own — plus your own custom exercises.

🗓️

Weekly plan

A routine per weekday, and any day can be rescheduled when life happens — without touching the plan.

⚖️

Body weight & goal

Interactive chart with your goal line; every change is colored by whether it moves toward it.

💪

Muscle map

A body diagram shaded by how much work each muscle got — and which ones got nothing.

🟩

Activity heatmap

A GitHub-style year view of your training time. Streaks included.

🔗

Supersets & cardio

Pair exercises back-to-back with one rest, and log time + speed where weight × reps makes no sense.

📥

Bring your history

Import from FitNotes, Strong, Hevy — or body weight straight out of an Apple Health export.

🌍

12 languages

Full UI translation, exercise instructions in 10 languages, loaded on demand.

🔑

Passkeys, not passwords

Self-hosted profiles sign in with Face ID / fingerprint. Each person's data stays their own.

🎨

Designed, not assembled

Light & dark themes, 8 accent colors, a hand-drawn icon set. Looks the same on every phone.

📦

Yours to keep

One-tap JSON export and import. Your training log is a file you own, not a row in someone's cloud.

Get openGym

One app, two flavors

Same codebase, your choice of where the data lives: entirely on your phone, or on a server you control.

📱 Mobile app

For anyone — install and you're done.

  • Everything stays on your phone
  • No account, no server, no setup
  • Native workout-day reminders
  • Backups via the share sheet
  • No cross-device sync
Download for Android

v1.2.2 · 4.4 MB · Android 6.0+ · signed APK, sideload (your browser will ask — that's normal outside the Play Store)

iPhone? Apple doesn't allow installs outside the App Store — use the self-hosted version as a home-screen app instead, or see the docs.

🖥️ Self-hosted

For homelabbers — and their friends & family.

  • Sync across all your devices
  • Passkey profiles for multiple people
  • Your data readable on the desktop
  • Works as a home-screen app on iPhone & Android
  • One docker compose up
Self-hosting guide

Why openGym exists

openGym started as a tiny app I built for myself — I just wanted to log my workouts and weight without a subscription app claiming my training data. Then friends wanted in, then it grew a mind of its own.

— Duarte Santos · read the whole story →
